DISCUSSION
A deeper low will dig into the area over the next 24 hours with additional cold air advection and gusty winds at times. Friday will be the coolest day for our corner of the state. We might see a few snow showers in the mountains, but little or no accumulation. Best precip chances will be north of our area. Seasonably cool temperatures overnight the next few nights.
